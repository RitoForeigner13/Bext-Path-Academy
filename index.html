About Us

Bext Path Academy is a leading Nursery, Primary and Secondary school in Kurudu, Abuja, committed to academic excellence, character development, and holistic education. Established in 2018, we have nurtured generations of leaders, innovators, and responsible citizens.

  • Read More

Why Choose Us?

  • Experienced Teachers: Our staff are qualified, passionate, and dedicated to each student's success.
  • Modern Facilities: Science labs, ICT center, library, sports fields, and smart classrooms.
  • Comprehensive Curriculum: Nigerian National Curriculum, WAEC/NECO preparation, and a range of extracurricular activities.
  • Safe and Inclusive Environment: We prioritize safety, discipline, and respect for all students.
  • Strong Community: Active parent-teacher association, alumni network, and community partnerships.
